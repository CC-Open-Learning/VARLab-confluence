Participants:

Recruit a diverse group of participants representing the target user demographic. Aim for a mix of experience levels, including both novice (students) and experienced users (students/alumni/industry experts).

  • CE

  • Students

  • Alumni

  • Industry Experts

Testing Methodology:

1. Moderated Usability Testing:

  • Conduct in-person* or remote moderated usability testing sessions.

  • Utilize a usability testing script to guide participants through common tasks.

  • Observe participants' interactions with the software and gather real-time feedback.

2. Unmoderated Remote Testing:

  • Implement an online platform to facilitate unmoderated remote usability testing.

  • Distribute tasks and scenarios to participants and collect feedback asynchronously.

  • Use screen recording and session analytics to analyze user interactions.

*in-person : Book rooms for testing or library rooms/ 3G-26

Testing Requirements:

1. In-person:

  • For Card sorts - stickie notes & pen

  • Focus group (5-6) - concept testing/qualitative interviews

  • Usability Testing: Paper prototype /playable demo/ Figma Wireframes

2. Remote:

  • Card sorts/tree testing - Optimal workshop / Figma /Twine

  • Focus group (5-6) - concept testing/qualitative interviews

  • Usability Testing: Figma wireframes, Grey Boxing 3d assets (in 3d environment), Text-based scenarios- Twine

3-week Implementation Timeline:

  • Preparation: Define test scenarios, recruit participants, and finalize testing protocols. (2 weeks)

  • Testing Execution: Conduct usability testing sessions and gather data.

  • Data Analysis: Analyze results, identify patterns, and prioritize issues.

  • Reporting: Create a usability testing report and share findings with stakeholders.

Recruiting channels:

  • Screening surveys through Conestoga college portal

  • Grassroot recruiting - Through the department chair / Instructors - CE

  • Set up a stall at college for volunteers to test - in a noisy environment or library

Incentives:

  • $25 gift cards

  • Free Lunch/snacks for in-person focus groups & participatory workshops

  • Short term contracts / Stipend for participants
