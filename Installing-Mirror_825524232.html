VARLab : Installing Mirror

Created by Talon Ernst, last modified on Oct 13, 2023

This document will cover the installation process of Mirror.

The first step is to Add Mirror to your Unity Assets.

Navigate to this link. Login if required. And hit the “Add to my Assets” button, as shown in Figure 1

NOTE: My button says “Open in Unity” because I already have it added. If you do not have it added yet it will say “Add to my Assets”.

You now have Mirror added to your Unity assets! Now, you can go to the project to which you want to add it.

Once you are in your project. Navigate to the “Window” tab in the top navigation bar. As shown in Figure 2. open it.

Once you have this tab open. Navigate to “Package Manager” as shown in Figure 3. Open it.

Next, when the package manager opens. Click on the button that says “Packages: Unity Registry” near the top left in the navigation bar, and change it to “My Assets”, as seen in Figure 4.

NOTE: If you get a unity error or the tab won’t switch to “My Assets”. You are either not logged in to Unity, or need to logout and login again to authenticate.

Once you switch it to “My Assets”, you should see “Mirror” in the list now! All that is left to do is click on Mirror in the list of assets and click “Import” in the bottom right. This can be seen in Figure 5.

Once you hit “Import”, a small window box will appear where you can pick exactly what you want to import. We do not need to exclude anything, so just hit “Import” again on this new window, as seen in Figure 6.

Once the installation process is complete, you are done installing Mirror! Congratulations. You are now ready to make a multiplayer game!
