VARLab : Licensing for Agisoft Metashape

Created by estronge, last modified on Jun 07, 2024

Current Recommendation

I have confirmed via the sales team at Agisoft that we are eligible for the Educational license for now. I would recommend getting an Educational Node locked Standard Edition of the software while we are still in the research phase and changing to Non-Educational Standard Edition if needed if we eventually decide to use it for commercial purposes.

The professional edition primarily adds functionalities relating to Aerial Mapping and landscape surface construction, which isn’t necessary for our project, and is such a huge price increase that upgrading just to try it out isn’t a beneficial route to take.
