Main Goal of the QA Team in VARLab

The primary objective of the QA team in VARLab is to support the organization by improving existing testing processes. Our efforts are focused on:

  • Enhancing current processes to streamline testing and ensure efficiency.

  • Assisting the teams in identifying challenges and areas for improvement in their workflows.

This Confluence space is maintained as a central knowledge repository for both current team members and other teams. It documents our initiatives, including:

  • Steps were taken to transition from the existing process to the new, improved workflow.

  • Best practices, lessons learned, and key outcomes from these improvements.
