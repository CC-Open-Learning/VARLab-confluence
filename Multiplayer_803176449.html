VARLab : Multiplayer

Created by Talon Ernst, last modified on Sept 21, 2023

Multiplayer can be added to Unity with many frameworks in many different ways. The plan is to research and test many frameworks that provide multiplayer to determine the best fit for VARLab and what we should put more resources into in the future.

Some research on multiplayer has already been done. The research was on one potential option, Fish-Net. That research and documentation can be found here.
