VARLab : Pre-Onboarding Bitbucket and Atlassian

Created by User 888a8, last modified on Aug 31, 2022

This document will show the process of creating an account and accepting the invites to Bitbucket and Atlassian. The screenshots are from the perspective of creating a brand new account and joining the VARLab.

After going to bitbucket.org, and choosing to sign up(I also selected Jira Software) you should see the following after filling out your information:

After choosing agree, you should get an email that looks like this:

Next, you’ll be asked to create your password:

Then, you should accept the invitation for you to join Bitbucket that was sent out.

Here, it asked me to create a username:

This brought me to the home screen for Bitbucket

If after selecting the all workspaces option, you don’t see VARLab Conestoga as an option, then select the “Accept my invitation” option again. After doing so, you should be able to get to this screen:

From here, you should next be able to select this email:

After selecting “Join the team”, you should see this:

Don’t worry if you can’t see any repositories on Bitbucket or the services on Atlassian. That’s intentional, full permissions will be granted when the term starts.
