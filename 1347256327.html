General Notes

Equipment

It might be beneficial to have multiple computers so that we can focus on photogrammetry (a laptop isn‚Äôt essential for photogrammetry as all the uploading and Agisoft work happens back on campus) and scanning simultaneously. Having another one to take notes might also be a good idea.

Having a motorized turntable would be beneficial so that only one person is required for photogrammetry, leaving the other person to focus on scanning (either they scan the object and maneuver around it or spin a non-manual turntable at their discretion).

Space

Note: this was with two people operating the equipment and two other people floating around¬†

It seemed that we needed a 3m x 3m space (minimum) to set up the scanning equipment and medium-sized lightbox.

Note: We were not able to use both scanners at the same time due to computer and space restrictions.

We would most likely need a 6m x 6m space (minimum) for the 3d scanning and photogrammetry process to use the large lightbox with the scanning equipment.
