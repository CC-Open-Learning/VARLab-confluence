VARLab : Instructional Design Review Checklist

Created by Sana Javeed, last modified on Mar 10, 2025

This is the Checklist against which the instructional Designers will review with for their build review either for Sprint demo builds/ Milestone Release/ General Release

Contributors:

Review Categories 

Questions to Ask 

Alignment to learning outcomes 

Does the activity help students meet the intended outcomes? 

Meets learning purpose 

Does the amount of feedback match the intended teaching purpose? 

Does the amount of teaching content match the intended teaching purpose? 

Navigation 

Can students navigate the experience independently? 

Are more instructions needed? 

Is the navigation confusing? 

Is there a menu for keyboard controls? Are there too many keyboard controls? 

Audio/Visuals 

Do they distract the learner from the task? 

Can students control volume and sound effects? 

Pacing 

Is there an appropriate amount of time between instructions (do students have the right amount of time to move from one task to the next)? 

 

Reusability 

What features can encourage students to reuse the DLX (if that is the intention)?- ie pacing, gamification, multiple scenarios, etc. 

Bugs 

Do the features work as intended? 

Instructions 

Do students know what is expected of them? 

Are students able to repeat instructions, if needed? 

Can students access a menu, when needed? 

Accessibility/UDL 

Are students able to access the instructions in more than one way? 

Are there captions for audio? 

Is there audio for text on screen? 

Script 

Are there any spelling/grammar errors? 

DEIB 

How are characters being represented?
