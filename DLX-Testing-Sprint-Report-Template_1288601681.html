VARLab : DLX Testing Sprint Report Template

Created by Sana Javeed, last modified on Feb 18, 2025

Create a copy of this template for each DLX to report on the findings during a sprint demo

Document Version Number: VERSION_1

Goals:

Each development team will utilize a copy of the provided templates to document and showcase their testing results. The primary goal is to evaluate the findings, mitigate any risks or issues, and ensure optimized and user-centric experiences across all DLX projects by first identifying and addressing any potential concerns.

Objectives:

  1. Template Usage:

    • Make a copy of this template within your respective project DLX.

    • Populate the template with the observed results.

  2. Result Presentation:

    • Present these results during the demo sessions.

    • Provide a trend analysis over the development cycles during the successive sprint demos.

  3. Testing Approach: The testing process will follow a 3-tier approach, divided into:

    • Demo-Level Testing:Evaluate the parameters defined below during each sprint. Share the findings with the team after every sprint demo.

    • Early-Audit Testing: Conduct tests on the defined parameters to identify and address potential concerns while the project progresses to mitigate risks and ensure quality at an early stage.

    • Release Testing: Conduct a final comprehensive evaluation of the DLX to determine its readiness for release. Use the same parameters as in Early-Audit Testing to assess the DLX, leading to a PASS or FAIL decision for the release candidate.
