VARLab : Checklist for AMBLab visit at Doon (Field Test)

Created by User 96d6e, last modified on Sept 16, 2024

Best practice for using this checklist would be to check each item off when it is being brought to the location, and uncheck it when it is being unpacked back at VARLab.

DOUBLE CHECK that everything is brought to the site and that it returns to VARLab. Be extra mindful of the expensive toys.

Creality Raptor 3D Scanner

  • Main Case
    • Calibration Mat (Dots)
    • Extra Identifier Dots (in a plastic bag)
    • Creality Handheld Scanner
    • Power Cord
    • Connection Cable (Scanner to Computer and Scanner to Power Cord)
    • Lens Cloth

Einstar 3D Scanner

  • Main Case
    • Einstar Handheld Scanner
    • Power Cable
    • Connection Cable (Scanner to Computer and Scanner to Power Cord)
    • Extra Identifier Dots (in a plastic bag)
    • Calibration Plaque (Dots, in a black velvet bag)

      • Battery
    • Lens Cleaning Kit
    • Camera Charger
    • SD Card Reader
  • Tripod Bag

    • Clear Case with the Boot for the Tripod
