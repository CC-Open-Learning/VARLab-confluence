VARLab : Tips for Image Capturing

Created by estronge, last modified on Jun 10, 2024

There are a variety of techniques that you can use to improve the output of a 3D scan. If you are having trouble getting an output of reasonable quality, or you are looking for a place to start, consider these tips:

Aim for

  • Completely even lighting

  • At least 70% overlap between adjacent images, unless software specifies compatibility with unordered lists

  • More photos 

  • No zoom on phone at all!

  • Use Transparency/Glossy Spray (We don’t have this yet, but eventually this could be important)

  • Close-ups to capture details

  • Object should take up the majority of the frame

Caution

  • Shadows

  • Obvious/out of place highlights

  • Carpet

  • Unwanted objects in the foreground

Avoid

  • Depth of field

  • Uneven ground

  • Geometrical Transformations of the image (Scale, Crop, Rotate)

  • Moving objects
