VARLab : How To Update Existing VARLab Content in Your Course

Created by Stacey Dineen, last modified on Jan 13, 2023

When a new version of your VARLab virtual learning content is available, you will be notified by your Chair. Virtual learning experiences are sometimes updated after initial release so that we can fix bugs, add new features, or improve the performance of the DLS package.

Remember:

You do not need to download or install anything.

Use a computer or laptop, not a phone or tablet. Touchscreen is not supported.

This will take less than 15 minutes.

No special computer skills or equipment are required.

To use the VARLab learning modules in your course, you need to copy them from VARLab’s model course into your own course. This document will provide step-by-step instructions.

Step by Step – Update existing VARLab content to your course:

Before you can add any new content to your course, you will need to hide or remove the old DLS content.

1. From your course, select the Content page and select the DLS content module. Click the down arrow beside the module title, and select either ‘Hide from users' or 'Delete module’. Confirm your selection, if required. Either option is fine, it is entirely up to you.

2. Now that the old content is hidden or removed, you can add the new version of the DLS content to your course. Open the Course Admin panel and select Import/Export/Copy Components. You will find this option in the Site Resources section of the Course Admin panel.

3. Under "Copy Components from another Org Unit", click the Search for Offering button.

4. Search for “VARLAB". From the search results, select the Offering Code that matches the one provided. The Offering Name will include VARLAB and the title will match your program. Click the box beside that course and click Add Selected.

After adding the components, click "Copy All Components" at the bottom of the page. This will take a few minutes, there is a progress bar to let you know how it's going.

Once you've got the contents into your own course shell, you can add or edit descriptions, dates, or instructions for your students as you see fit. Enrolled students will automatically see the DLS content the next time they login to your course!

PLEASE NOTE: These modules are currently set with NO grade value, so it's important not to mess with that. The digital learning modules will not report any grades to eConestoga, so if you set them as gradable, all students will get zero!
