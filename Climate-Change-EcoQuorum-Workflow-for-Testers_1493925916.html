User Flow Testing Process

Step 1 : Tutorial

  • Follow the instructions in the tutorial. When you are finished, teleporting (pressing T) will take you to the elevator, and clicking on the door will allow you to exit.  

Step 2 : Team Setup

  • You have been set up teams of 3 or 4. First player: begin the game by entering your name (7 max characters). This will launch a team name given to your team: share that name with your teammates. Teammates will then select that Team Name from the list to join that team. 

Step 3 : Elevator Video Intro

  • You will start in an elevator where you will learn about the critical climate change situation. You cannot do anything while the video plays. After the video stops, leave the elevator. (Use Navigation controls listed below) 

Step 4 : Phase 1 | Boardroom Sequence

  • You will enter the boardroom (after passing through the lounge). Here you will choose a chair that assigns your industry for the duration of gameplay.  

  • You will learn some initial details about choices to make, and you will make some individual (and later collaborative) decisions in the boardroom. You make choices by selecting an up or down arrow on the tablet screen, or by clicking on one of four options. 

Step 5 : Phase 2 | Office Sequence

  • You will leave the boardroom and move to your office door (labelled according to industry) to enter your office. You can only enter your own industry’s office.  

  • Here, you will learn see details on screen about an email, from which you’ll learn about a choice.  

  • Next, you’ll read about a situation affecting your industry and make a decision by clicking on one of four options. 

Step 6 : Phase 3 | Boardroom Sequence Return

  • Upon returning to the Boardroom, you’ll need to negotiate with your team about what you’ve chosen and why, and reaffirm the decision or reach a different one you all collectively agree to. It will follow the same process that was in your office, but working together to agree. 

  • You’ll need to be aware of your budget so you don’t go over. You’ll lock in those decisions, at which point Session 1 (2030) will finish. 

Step 7 , 8, 9 : Session 2 (2040)

  • will repeat the above sequences (4, 5, 6 with new decisions). 

Step 10 , 11, 12 : Session 3 (2050)

  • will repeat the above sequences (4, 5, 6 with new decisions). 

Step 13 : Results Page (After Session3 )

  • You will review results of your and your team’s contributions to the Climate Change crisis. You will learn how you did professionally, and according to climate change goals and financial ones.
