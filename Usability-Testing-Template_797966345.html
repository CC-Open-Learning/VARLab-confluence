User Research Questions

The Research Questions can be categorised under 3 groups : User Experience, Instructional design & Gaming elements

Ease of use & Navigation (User Experience)

How easy it is to use the interface in order to be successfully achieve the learning objectives?

Are users able to navigate while interacting with the 2D and 3D game elements? Is it easy to understand? And does it match the mental models of the users?

Promotes learning (Instructional Design)

Does the DLX promote the learning of the users which supplements the theoretical knowledge obtained in-class?

Does the DLX allow users to achieve the learning objectives that are defined in the course?

Engagement Factor (Game Design)

What is the engagement level that keeps the user motivated to achieve the required learning goals?

Are they any missing game elements that can promote the learning and engagement?

Session breakdown

Time for the Study- 30 mins - 40 mins

Activity

Description/Questions

Welcome

 (3 min)

Welcome and briefing on study objectives, permissions and rights.

Pre-task Interview

 (5 min)

Ice-breaker questions to verify user profile.

Task scenarios with post-task Interviews

 

(15 min)

3 scenario tasks, with 1 additional scenario task for students who are relocating from outside Ontario. There will be a short post-task interview at the end of each task.

Post-test Interview

 (8 min)

General discussion on the overall feel and sentiment of the product

Closing

 (3 min)

Debrief, Q&A and Thank you.
