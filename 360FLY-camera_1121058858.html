360FLY camera, a device from a company that died at least 5 years ago. It is somewhat akin to the owl camera, just older and no longer supported. The only place where any hope of support exists is on reddit.

Usage

  1. Hold the button to turn it on

  2. Set the camera down and wait for it to stop flashing'

  3. Press the button once to start recording

  4. Press the button again to stop recording

I don't know if its actually possible to take a still photo with this anymore, I think that relied on the mobile app that is long dead and broken.

Connecting to PC

To get videos off of the camera, plug the cradle stored in the bottom of the container into the PC. Set the camera on the cradle. The triangle should face away from the cord, otherwise the magnets will prevent it from connecting. Once you get it right it should snap into place with the magnets. When it connects it should open a folder on your desktop like connecting any camera, and you can pull your video out of the DICM subfolder.
